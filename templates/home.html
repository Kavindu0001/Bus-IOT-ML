System Overview

Our Travel Agent Supporting Tool integrates multiple advanced features to provide a complete solution for modern travel agencies. From passenger safety monitoring to driver assessment and profit optimization, this tool ensures operational excellence while maintaining the highest safety standards. The system uses AI and machine learning to provide real-time insights and automated recommendations.

Main Features
Passenger Anomaly Detection
Advanced AI-powered system that detects appearance changes and suspicious behaviour in passengers using GAN technology in real-time.
Driver Anomaly Detection
Monitor driver behaviour in real-time. Detects fatigue, distraction, and unsafe driving patterns to ensure high quality standards.
Profit Assessment
Comprehensive profit analysis and optimization tool. Calculates operational costs and predicts profitability for route optimization.
Other Features
Additional tools including travel history analysis, reporting systems, maintenance scheduling, and customer feedback management.
